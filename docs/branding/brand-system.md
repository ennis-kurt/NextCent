# Personal Accountant AI Brand System

## Positioning

**An AI personal accountant for everyday financial health.**

The product emphasizes financial clarity, practical next actions, debt and cash-flow guidance, spending awareness, and privacy-first AI.

## Voice and tone

- calm
- sharp
- practical
- plainspoken
- precise
- non-judgmental

Avoid:

- hype language
- fake certainty
- salesy fintech slang
- debt shaming
- cheerful gamification

## Visual system

- strong neutrals with paper-toned surfaces
- muted teal primary accent for trust and control
- brass support accent for analytical emphasis
- restrained semantic colors for positive, warning, and risk states
- generous spacing and crisp executive dashboard hierarchy
- `Manrope` for display, `IBM Plex Sans` for UI, `IBM Plex Mono` for numbers

## UX writing principles

- Explain before you overwhelm.
- Separate recommendations from raw numbers.
- State assumptions when the system is forecasting.
- Use calm language for risks.
- Make privacy visible.

## Copy patterns

### Safe to Spend

- “You have about $120 left for discretionary spending this week.”
- “At your current pace, that may run out by Thursday.”
- “This number is based on current balances, upcoming bills, debt minimums, and recent spending pace.”

### Recommendation cards

- What happened
- Why it matters
- What to do next
- Expected benefit

### Privacy

- “Raw account identifiers and transaction descriptions are blocked from external model payloads.”
- “Deterministic financial calculations happen before any model boundary.”
- “Recommendations are based on available account data and estimates, not regulated financial advice.”
