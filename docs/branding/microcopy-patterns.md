# UX Copy Patterns

## Dashboard summaries

- “Your checking buffer looks tight before the next paycheck.”
- “Dining spend is running above your recent pattern.”
- “Paying this card first would likely reduce next month’s interest.”

## Safe to Spend

- “You have about $120 left for discretionary spending this week.”
- “At your current pace, that may run out by Thursday.”
- “This number is not your balance. It is the amount that appears safe after bills, minimums, and buffer needs.”

## Debt optimizer

- “This strategy likely saves the most interest.”
- “This strategy keeps more cash available.”
- “This strategy may feel more achievable because it closes smaller balances first.”

## Simulation

- “This appears manageable, but it would leave a much thinner monthly cushion.”
- “This move likely improves debt payoff speed, but raises short-term cash pressure.”

## Subscription actions

- “This looks like a recurring subscription.”
- “You may want to review whether you still use it.”
- “Here is a draft message you can edit before sending.”

## Empty and loading states

- “Analysis is still building. The dashboard will populate after the first financial pass finishes.”
- “There is not enough recent history yet to estimate Safe to Spend confidently.”
- “AI explanation is temporarily unavailable. Deterministic financial insights are still available.”
